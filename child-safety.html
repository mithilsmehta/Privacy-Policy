LinkUp Child Safety Standards

LinkUp is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Prohibited Content

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Grooming, predatory, or inappropriate communication involving minors
  • Any content promoting harm to children

Violations will result in immediate account suspension and permanent removal.

Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content inside the app or contact us at: appchat91@gmail.com

Enforcement & Cooperation

We use moderation systems and cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities to prevent child exploitation.
