LinkUp Child Safety Standards

LinkUp is committed to maintaining a safe and secure environment for all users. We have a strict zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any activity involving the abuse, exploitation, or endangerment of minors is strictly prohibited on our platform.

1. Prohibited Content and Conduct

LinkUp strictly prohibits the following:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) in any form
  • Sexual exploitation or solicitation of minors
  • Grooming, predatory, or manipulative behavior toward minors
  • Inappropriate communication involving minors
  • Any content that promotes, facilitates, or encourages harm to children

Any user found engaging in such activities will be subject to immediate account suspension and permanent removal from the platform.

2. Reporting Mechanism

Users can report inappropriate content or suspicious behavior directly within the LinkUp app through the in-app reporting features. All reports are reviewed promptly and appropriate action is taken, which may include content removal, account suspension, or permanent banning.

Users may also report concerns by contacting us at: appchat91@gmail.com

3. Moderation and Preventative Measures

LinkUp uses moderation systems, monitoring tools, and appropriate technical measures to detect, prevent, and address content related to child exploitation. We continuously work to improve our detection and safety enforcement processes.

Accounts found violating our Child Safety Standards will be permanently banned and may be reported to relevant authorities.

4. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

LinkUp fully cooperates with law enforcement authorities and complies with all applicable local, national, and international laws regarding child protection. When legally required, we report suspected child exploitation activities to appropriate authorities.

5. Age Restrictions

LinkUp is not directed toward children under the age of 13. Users must meet the minimum age requirement as defined in our app listing and applicable laws.
