# LinkUp Child Safety Standards LinkUp is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). ## Prohibited Content LinkUp strictly prohibits: - Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) - Sexual exploitation of minors - Grooming, predatory, or inappropriate communication involving minors - Any content that promotes or facilitates harm to children Any violation will result in immediate account suspension and permanent removal from the platform. ## Reporting Mechanism Users can report inappropriate content or suspicious activity directly within the LinkUp app. We review all reports promptly and take appropriate action, including removal of content and banning of users. ## Moderation & Enforcement LinkUp actively monitors user activity and uses safety moderation practices to detect and prevent child exploitation. Accounts found violating our policies will be permanently banned. ## Cooperation with Law Enforcement LinkUp fully cooperates with law enforcement authorities and complies with all applicable laws related to child safety. ## Contact Information If you have concerns regarding child safety or suspect exploitation, please contact us at: appchat91@gmail.com
