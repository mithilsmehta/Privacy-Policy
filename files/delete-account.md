LinkUp respects user privacy and provides a simple way to delete your account and associated data.

## How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your LinkUp account:

1. Send an email to: **appchat91@gmail.com**
2. Use the subject line: **Account Deletion Request**
3. Include the email or username associated with your LinkUp account

## What Data Is Deleted

Once your request is processed, the following data is permanently deleted:
- User account information (username, email)
- Profile details
- Chat messages and conversation history

## Data Retention

- Backup data (if any) is automatically removed within **30 days**
- No data is retained beyond this period

## Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within **7 business days**.
